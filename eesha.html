Diwali

Diwali (also known as the festival of lights

It is celebrated to welcome home the gods Ram and Sita, after the exile

Where Diwali is celebrated

  • India
  • America
  • United Kingdom

Days of Diwali

  1. Dhanteras (buy gold/utensils)
  2. Choti Diwali (decoration with rangoli)
  3. DIWALI!! (prayers,rituals,fireworks!)
  4. Govardhan Puja (gift exchange)
  5. Bhai dooj (sisters promise to preotect their brother)
