Diwali

Diwali, also known as the Festival of Lights, is a major holiday celebrated by millions of people around the world. It symbolizes the victory of light over darkness and good over evil. Diwali is celebrated with lamps, candles, food, and time spent with family and friends.

  • India
  • Nepal
  • Sri Lanka
  • United Kingdom
  1. Homes are cleaned and decorated with lights and candles.
  2. Families create rangoli designs using colored powders.
  3. Oil lamps called diyas are lit in the evening.
  4. Prayers are offered to Lakshmi, the goddess of wealth.
  5. Families share sweets and exchange gifts.
