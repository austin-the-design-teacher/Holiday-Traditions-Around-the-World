What is Diwali and why is it celebrated?

Diwali is the hindu festival of lights and celebrates the win of good over evil. Diwali is a 5 day celebration, but the third day is the most important. The third day is Diwali and comemerates the day King Rama returns from his exil with his wife and brother after killing the demon Ravaan.
